Why We Started

Booking travel shouldn't feel like a transaction with an algorithm. We noticed that major portals were charging exorbitant convenience fees, offering robotic customer support, and leaving travelers stranded during emergencies.

That's why we created YatraBot. Operating directly out of Gujarat, India, we combined official wholesale travel inventory with the world's most accessible communication tool — WhatsApp. No apps, no hidden fees, just pure personal service.
